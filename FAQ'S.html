Frequently Asked Questions About swatch bharath

1, What is Swachh Bharat Abhiyan?

A. Swachh Bharat Abhiyan, also known as the Clean India Mission, is a nationwide cleanliness campaign launched by the Indian government on 2nd October 2014. It aims to make India clean and open defecation-free, ensuring improved sanitation facilities and hygiene practices across the country

2,What is the role of the government in Swachh Bharat Abhiyan?

A. The government plays a crucial role in Swachh Bharat Abhiyan by:
1. Implementing policies and programs to achieve the objectives of the campaign.
2. Allocating funds and resources for the construction of toilets and other sanitation facilities.
3. Monitoring the progress of the campaign and evaluating its impact.
4. Enforcing regulations and laws related to cleanliness, waste management, and sanitation.
5. Collaborating with various stakeholders, including local authorities and NGOs, to ensure effective implementation.

3, How has Swachh Bharat Abhiyan improved public health?

A. Swachh Bharat Abhiyan has had a positive impact on public health by:
1. Reducing the spread of water-borne diseases through improved sanitation practices.
2. Encouraging proper waste management, preventing the accumulation of waste and the breeding of disease-carrying vectors.
3. Promoting the use of toilets, which helps prevent contamination of water sources and reduces the risk of infections.
4. Creating awareness about hygiene practices, such as handwashing, which helps prevent the spread of diseases.
5. Overall improvement in cleanliness and hygiene standards, leading to a healthier environment for communities.

4, What are the objectives of Swachh Bharat Abhiyan?

A. The main objectives of Swachh Bharat Abhiyan are:
1. Eliminate open defecation and promote the use of toilets.
2. Provide access to safe and adequate sanitation facilities for all.
3. Create awareness about the importance of cleanliness and hygiene.
4. Eradicate manual scavenging.
5. Promote solid waste management and effective waste disposal.

5, What are the key achievements of Swachh Bharat Abhiyan?

A.Swachh Bharat Abhiyan has made significant progress since its launch. Some key achievements include:
1. Construction of millions of toilets across the country, reducing open defecation.
2. Increase in the percentage of households with access to improved sanitation facilities.
3. Expansion of sanitation coverage in rural areas.
4. Reduction in the number of open defecation spots and improvement in overall cleanliness.
5. Encouraging behavioral change and promoting cleanliness as a social norm.
